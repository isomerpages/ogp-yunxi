<h2><strong>Web Development Bootcamp</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Most of the online Web Development course is hosted on <strong>Udemy</strong>,
accessible via LEARN here: <a href="https://learncsc.udemy.com/course/the-complete-web-development-bootcamp/learn/lecture/38516770%23overview" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>https://learncsc.udemy.com/course/the-complete-web-development-bootcamp/learn/lecture/38516770#overview</u></a>.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>All modules in bold are mandatory to complete a web app. </strong>Modules
that are unbolded are optional but <u>highly recommended</u> for hands-on
practice before building your web app. All other sections that are not
listed in the table are also optional.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
